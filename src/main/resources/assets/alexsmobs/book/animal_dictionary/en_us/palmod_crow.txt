PAL STATS - Health: 8 | Attack: 1
<NEWLINE>
PAL ABILITY - Trader. Place its Work Station, then drop payment items (emeralds) on the ground nearby. The crow converts them into goods stored at the station.
<NEWLINE>
WILD VARIANT - Skittish. A wild catchable one flees the moment it sees or hears you. Sneak to quarter its detection range before throwing a sphere.
<NEWLINE>
CATCHING - Catch rate scales with sphere tier and the Leveling enchant, and rises steeply as the target is weakened. Only mobs with an enchanted shimmer can be caught - a Pal Compass points to the nearest one.
