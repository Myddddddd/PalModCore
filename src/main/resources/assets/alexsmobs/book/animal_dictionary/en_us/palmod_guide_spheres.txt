Pal Spheres are your catching tool. Throw one at a wild creature to try to catch it.
<NEWLINE>
Three tiers exist: the Pal Sphere (catch level 1), the Mid Pal Sphere (level 5) and the Advanced Pal Sphere (level 10). A higher tier catches tougher creatures.
<NEWLINE>
Right-click a creature up close to throw point-blank. Only creatures that shimmer faintly can be caught - a sphere thrown at anything else is lost.
