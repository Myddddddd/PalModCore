In the wild, tougher creatures fight back. They come in four types:
<NEWLINE>
Brawler - tanky and hits hard.
<NEWLINE>
Skittish - flees the moment it sees or hears you; sneak to get close.
<NEWLINE>
OP - freezes or dodges your spheres.
<NEWLINE>
Predator-locked - beaten only with the right counter Pal, or by luring it out of hiding.
