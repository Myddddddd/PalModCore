Some Pals can run a Work Station. Sneak and use a station-type Pal on a block to place its station - the Pal appears and starts working.
<NEWLINE>
Jobs include Harvester, Lumberjack, Miner, Trader and Sorter.
<NEWLINE>
Right-click the station to collect what the Pal produced. Break it to get the Pal's sphere back.
