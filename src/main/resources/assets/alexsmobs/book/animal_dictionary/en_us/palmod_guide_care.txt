<NEWLINE>
A summoned Pal grows Hungry and Moody over time.
<NEWLINE>
Place a Pal Feeder and stock it with food; hungry Pals visit it to eat. You can also feed a Pal by hand - right-click it while holding its food.
<NEWLINE>
A starving Pal slows down and stops working, so keep the feeder full. A released Pal stays in the world only while its sphere is in your hotbar or offhand.
